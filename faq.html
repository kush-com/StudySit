Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about StudySit.

1. What is StudySit? +
StudySit is a modern co-working and study space designed for students, freelancers, and professionals. You can book seats, desks, or private offices on an hourly, weekly, or monthly basis at your convenience.
2. How do I book a workspace? +
You can easily book your seat or office through our website using the “Book Now” button. It redirects you to our Google Form for quick registration and confirmation.
3. Are there hourly or monthly passes available? +
Yes, StudySit offers flexible pricing plans such as hourly, weekly, and monthly passes depending on your needs.
4. What amenities are included with my booking? +
All members enjoy superfast Wi-Fi, comfortable seating, charging points, air conditioning, coffee & refreshments, and a community-driven environment that fosters focus and collaboration.
5. Can I bring food or beverages? +
Yes, you can bring your own snacks and beverages. We also have an in-house café serving coffee and refreshments.
